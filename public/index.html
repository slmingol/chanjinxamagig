How to Play

What is a Word Ladder?

A word ladder is a puzzle where you transform one word into another by changing one letter at a time. Each step must be a valid word.

For example, to get from CAT to DOG:

CAT → COT → DOT → DOG

How to Solve

  1. Read the clues - They're shown out of order on the right side
  2. Fill in the ladder - Type words into the empty rungs
  3. Each word must differ by exactly one letter from the word above it
  4. Use hints sparingly - Click the 💡 button to reveal which clue applies to that rung
  5. Check your solution - When complete, see your path and share your results!

Tips

  • Clues reference words you've already solved using ____ for unsolved words
  • The letter change indicator (like E→O) appears after you solve each word
  • Try to solve without hints for a better completion percentage!
  • Use the ← → arrows to navigate between different puzzles

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • ↑ / ↓ - Navigate between ladder rungs
  • H - Show hint for current rung (press H or Enter again to confirm)
  • Enter - Move to next rung or confirm hint
  • Escape - Close hint dialog

About CAT·CLIMBER

CAT·CLIMBER is inspired by Raddle.quest by The Mystery League. It's a daily word ladder puzzle game where cats climb their way from one word to another!
